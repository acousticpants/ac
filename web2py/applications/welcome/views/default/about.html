Why does this site even exist?

It doesn't do anything particularly special. Right?

Well...yes. But most of the internet doesn't do anything special. Apart from the porn,
downloading movies, tv, games, and stalking each other on facebook, one could say there
is really very little on the web which makes a difference.

Fair enough I say, fair enough. I'll cut to the chase.

I quite like science. Like, a lot. Molecules, and maths, and charts of data, and
obscure terminology. It really gets me going. I also like I.T. (as in Information
Technology). So this site is a kind of attempt at throwing the two together. I've
noticed there are a LOT of programs out there for molecular modelling, simulation,
viewig and animation.

The home page on this site gives you a famous example of one these programs. 'Jmol'.

There are many of them. Too many, in my humble opinion. So I think I'd like to try
and simplify things for people. Why should you have to install all theses programs,
and then run them, and pay for a computer powerful enough to run them? I don't think
it's an efficient approach.

So I want to make a website which can do these kinds of simulations and modelling,
and then make the website do all the work, rather than your computer. This makes a
lot of sense, because you can just go the website, get your molecules to do the
things you want, and you can just do that from your laptop, or even your smartphone.

This is pretty much why this site exists. It's a work in progress. It will take time.
I have no idea what I'm doing. But I'll figure it out along the way.
